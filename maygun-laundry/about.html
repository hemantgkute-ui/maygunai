Who We Are

Clean and Fresh, By Design

Maygun Laundry was founded with a simple idea — laundry day shouldn't be a chore. From your everyday shirts to delicate sarees, woolens, shoes and household linens, we treat every item with the same care we'd give our own.

Based in Nagpur, we combine modern fabric-care techniques with old-fashioned attention to detail. Every garment is inspected, sorted, and cleaned using the right process for its fabric — then pressed, packed and delivered straight to your door.

Our name reflects our promise: Maygun, for meticulous care, and Laundry done the way it should be — thorough, gentle, and always fresh.

Our Process

How We Work

Every order follows the same careful, quality-checked journey from pickup to delivery.

1. Booking

You book via WhatsApp or our online form, choosing your preferred pickup date and service.

2. Pickup & Sorting

Our team collects your items and sorts them by fabric type and cleaning method.

3. Cleaning & Quality Check

Each item is cleaned, treated for stains if needed, and inspected before finishing.

4. Pressing & Packing

Garments are steam-pressed, folded or hung, and neatly packed for delivery.

5. Doorstep Delivery

Your fresh, clean order is delivered back to you — free of charge, right on schedule.
